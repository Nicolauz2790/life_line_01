## Usage Tips

### Managing Subscriptions
1. Click "Add Subscription" to add a new service
2. Fill in the service name, category, cost, and billing cycle
3. Set the next renewal date to get timely alerts
4. Mark subscriptions as inactive when you cancel them (keeps history)

### Tracking Accounts
1. Click "Add Account" to register a new online account
2. Enter service name, username, and email
3. Enable the 2FA checkbox if you have it set up
4. Update the password change date whenever you change passwords
5. Review security warnings regularly

### Digital Assets
1. Click "Add Asset" to register a digital property
2. Choose the appropriate asset type
3. Enter estimated value for portfolio tracking
4. Set renewal dates for domains, certificates, etc.
5. Keep notes about access details and configurations

### Security Best Practices
- Aim for a security score above 70%
- Enable 2FA on all critical accounts (banking, email, social media)
- Change passwords every 6 months (180 days)
- Review security recommendations weekly
- Keep your digital asset renewals up to date

## Data Privacy

All data is stored locally in your Life Ledger database. No information is sent to external servers. The application does NOT store actual passwords - only metadata about your accounts and security status.
